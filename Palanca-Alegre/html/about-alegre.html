Alegre (est. 2019)

Alegre is the last pioneering batch of the Philippine Science High School - Zamboanga Peninsula Region Campus, the bridge between corridors of the Olingan Sports Complex and the hallways of the official campus. "Alegre," quite a simple name, but happiness is part of many memories of this batch. By being many of ZRC's firsts and lasts, Alegre continues to shine with beaming smiles and a steady heart towards the future. Here's to more memories, and here's to being Iskolars ng Bayan keme keme
